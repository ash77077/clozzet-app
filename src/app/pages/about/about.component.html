Our Mission

To empower businesses to express their unique identity through sustainable, high-quality branded apparel. We are committed to delivering innovative textile solutions that blend creativity, functionality, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the leading B2B textile partner, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer success. We envision a future where every business can showcase their brand with confidence through our exceptional products.

Our Story

From a small startup to a trusted B2B textile partner

Clozzet was born from a simple observation: businesses needed a reliable partner who could deliver high-quality, branded apparel that truly represented their values and identity. Founded in 2018 by industry veterans with a combined 40+ years of experience, we set out to revolutionize the B2B textile industry.

What started as a small operation with a handful of local clients has grown into a comprehensive textile solutions provider serving over 1,000 businesses nationwide. Our success is built on three core principles: unwavering quality, exceptional customer service, and innovative solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted partner for companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, helping them build stronger teams and brands through professionally crafted apparel.
